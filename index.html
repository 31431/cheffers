Features
Cheffers connects aspiring home chefs with adventurous foodies. Home chefs list their specialties on our platform to offer their creations to foodies searching for an experience different from what you can get at a store or restaurant. Hungry users can connect with home chefs near them to order a freshly prepade, delicious hot meal. All deliveries are made by partners, ensuring your meal gets delivered right to your doorstep.

About Us
Every family has a secret recipe, designed to please the palatte. Some are traditions passed down through generations, while others are new inventions concocted by aspiring home-chefs. Unfortunately, most of us may never get invited to try these amazing dishes. Cheffers uses technology to make these home cooked meals available to the public by connecting home chefs to the food lovers looking for unique culinary experiences.
